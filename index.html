May Mental Health Bingo Card Tracker

This May, take small steps toward improving your mental well-being. Each square on this bingo card is a chance to prioritize yourself, find joy, and embrace positivity. Remember, even small efforts can make a big difference.

Follow these steps to complete your Bingo Card:

  1. Click on a square to mark it as completed.
  2. When you get a bingo (a full row, column, or diagonal is completed), click the "Submit Bingo Card" button.
  3. A PDF will be generated and automatically downloaded to your computer.
  4. An email draft will open. Please attach the generated PDF to the email.
  5. Hit send to submit your bingo card!
