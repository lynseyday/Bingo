May Mental Health Bingo Card Tracker

Follow these steps to complete your Bingo Card:

  1. Click on a square to mark it as completed.
  2. When you get a bingo (a full row, column, or diagonal is completed), click the "Check for Bingo" button.
  3. A PDF will be generated and automatically downloaded to your computer.
  4. An email draft will open. Please attach the generated PDF to the email.
  5. Hit send to submit your bingo card!
